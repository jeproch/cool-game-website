Plumber vs Cook

The best 2D game for all people to enjoy together

Plumber vs. Cook" is an exciting 2D combat game that pits two unlikely rivals against each other in a hilarious and action-packed showdown. Join Plunger, a fearless plumber armed with an arsenal of plumbing tools, and Cook, a nimble and resourceful chef wielding kitchen utensils like never before, as they engage in a quirky battle for supremacy. With fast-paced gameplay, a variety of unique abilities, and a quirky sense of humor, "Plumber vs. Cook" promises a fun-filled gaming experience that will keep you entertained for hours. Choose your side, master your character's skills, and get ready for an epic battle that will challenge your wits and reflexes in this comically epic clash of trades.

Plumber vs Cook started out as a project to learn game development
